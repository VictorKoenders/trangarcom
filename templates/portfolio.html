CoolerBot

CoolerMaster wanted to increase their customer engagement. As their customers were mostly gamers, CoolerMaster approached Trangar to talk about the possibilities of creating a chatbot on Discord, a platform used by gamers to chat with each other. The project resulted in 3 different portals, aimed at the different users of the system, as well as a standalone application that handled the interaction with Discord.

You can check out the bot in CoolerMaster's Discord channel.
